How about to track your teamwork?

Does your team work with multiple repositories? Keeping track of what's happening with all of them can be time consuming and frustrating sometimes. TeamTracker provides a lightweight way for your team to be aware of what's going on with multiple repositories and gives you insights on how your team collaborates. TeamTracker currently integrates with GitHub and Slack to get information about how your team communicates, how your projects are progressing and what the other members of your team are doing.

It's being developed by Coopera, a research group from Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Norte.

Who can use it?

TeamTracker can be used by all kinds of software development groups, small or big, like:

  • Students working on a course project.
  • Small development teams
  • Big enterprises with multiple development teams
  • Open source teams.
